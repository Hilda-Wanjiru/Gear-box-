Hello World! I am Hilda Wanjiru Ng'ang'a, a final year student at the University of Nairobi, Faculty of Engineering, Department of Environmental and Biosystems Engineering. I am glad to be among the final year students chosen by the African Engineering and Technology Network (AFRETEC) in cooperation with Gearbox to be a part of this training. My expectation is to learn about embedded systems functionality and their applications to help advance my career.

My Final Year project is the Design of a biogas plant with floral waste feedstock. My incorporation of the embedded systems is in the temperature, pH of the biodigester to increase efficiency and improve the quality of biogas produced.
